About Me

Hello! My name is Derek and I enjoy making robots come to life. My interest in robotics started back in 2018 when I decided to try joining my High School robotics club — who knew that this would be the start of my robotics journey.

Fast-forward to today, and I’ve had the privilege of working at an advertising agency, a start-up, a huge corporation, and a student-led design studio. As a senior Robotics Engineering student at the University of Michigan-Dearborn, I am passionate about leveraging advanced technology to create innovative solutions in automation and robotics. My academic journey has equipped me with a solid foundation through hands-on projects. I've competed in national robotics competitions, conducted autonomous robotics research, and currently serve on the executive board of the Intelligent Systems Club. I'm passionate about photography and web design, bringing creativity and technical expertise to each project.

I also recently launched a course that covers everything you need to build a web app with the Spotify API using Node & React.
